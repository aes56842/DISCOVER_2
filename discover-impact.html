BROADENING PARTICIPATION IN COMPUTING

Superconductor electronics hasn't had its breakout moment yet. That means there's still room to shape the field.

DISCoVER is a seven-university collaboration built on the belief that better hardware comes from a wider range of people building it. We open our research, mentorship, and community to undergraduates, graduate students and postdocs, faculty, and anyone curious about the future of computing.

What We're Building Toward

Five commitments that guide how DISCoVER works, not just what it researches.

01

Broaden participation at every career stage

From first research experiences as an undergraduate through the transition into a faculty career, DISCoVER builds pathways rather than one-off opportunities — including joint majors, M.S. research scholar tracks, and mentoring into the professoriate.

02

Open, collaborative research

Tools, methods, and findings are shared openly across our seven partner institutions and beyond, so people outside the immediate collaboration can build on the work.

03

Equal access to opportunity

We study the barriers that keep talented people out of computing research and act on what we find — from redesigning intro coursework to partnering directly with HBCUs, HSIs, and other minority-serving institutions.

04

A culture of collaboration across difference

Progress in superconductor electronics depends on people with different training and perspectives working together — across institutions, disciplines, and backgrounds.

05

Public engagement, not just publication

Research findings reach the public through an open seminar series and ongoing outreach, not only through journals and conferences.

Ways to Get Involved

Whatever stage you're at, there's a way in.

Undergraduate Students

  • Paid research experiences in DISCoVER labs, including through partnerships with existing NSF REU programs
  • Introductory CS coursework redesigned to build confidence and community regardless of background
  • Joint majors pairing CS with fields like biology or business
  • The annual DISCoVER Undergraduate BPC Conference, where past participants share their experience with the next cohort

Graduate Students & Postdocs

  • New M.S. Research Scholars pathways encouraging students from underserved communities toward doctoral study
  • Direct mentoring on academic careers, including workshops for underrepresented senior graduate students
  • The annual DISCoVER Graduate (and Beyond) BPC Conference for sharing ideas across institutions
  • A structured pipeline into postdoctoral and early-career faculty roles

Faculty

  • Cross-institutional collaboration spanning devices, circuits, architecture, and applications across seven universities
  • Kickoff and year-three workshops to shape joint research directions with colleagues across academia and industry
  • Opportunities to mentor early-career faculty and expand the pipeline to the professoriate
  • Access to shared tools, datasets, and open-source research infrastructure
OPEN TO EVERYONE

Everyone Has Value.

Superconductor electronics will shape how AI, medicine, and climate science scale in the next decade. We think that's worth explaining clearly, not just publishing quietly — so a meaningful part of DISCoVER's work is making the research legible to anyone who's curious.
